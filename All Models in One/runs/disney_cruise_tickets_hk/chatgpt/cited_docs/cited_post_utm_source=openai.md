**Now Is the Perfect Time to Sail**

![Disney Cruise line deals — Captain Minnie Mouse waving on stage at the Sail Away Party](https://cache.undercovertourist.com/blog/2025/09/0925-disney-treasure-sail-away-party.jpg)

[There’s a lot happening with Disney Cruise Line](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/whats-coming-disney-cruise-line/) right now, which makes it a great time to start planning a cruise vacation! Bluey and Bingo now appear onboard the [Disney Dream](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/overview-disney-dream/) with interactive activities and dance parties. The experience will also be available on the [Disney Wish](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/disney-wish/) beginning in June 2026.

The past few years have brought major updates and new destinations. In June 2024, Disney Cruise Line opened its second island destination, [Lookout Cay at Lighthouse Point](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/disney-lookout-cay-at-lighthouse-point/). The Disney Dream also received several upgrades, including updates to Disney’s Oceaneer Club, a new funnel suite, a new concierge lounge and a refreshed spa.

![Disney Cruise line deals — Plaza de Coco Stage Show](https://cache.undercovertourist.com/blog/2025/03/0325-plaza-de-coco-stage-show.jpg)

Disney Cruise Line has also expanded its fleet. The [Disney Treasure](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/disney-treasure-cruise-faqs/), the second Wish Class ship, launched in December 2024 with adventure-inspired design, themed lounges, including the ghostly [Haunted Mansion Parlor](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/haunted-mansion-parlor-disney-treasure-ship/) and a new Rotational Dining restaurant, Plaza de Coco.

In November 2025, the [Disney Destiny](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/disney-destiny/) debuted from Fort Lauderdale, sailing to the Bahamas and Western Caribbean with stops at Disney Castaway Cay and Disney Lookout Cay.

And just since March, the [Disney Adventure](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/disney-adventure/) began sailing from Singapore, with “Magic at Sea” cruises and brand-new themed areas inspired by Disney, Pixar and Marvel.

![Disney Cruise line deals — Family Beach at Lookout Cay](https://cache.undercovertourist.com/blog/2025/01/01-25-lookout-cay-beach.jpg)

No matter the [Disney Cruise Line ship](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/need-to-know-disney-cruise-line/) or [itinerary](https://www.undercovertourist.com/blog/disney-cruise-line-itineraries/), each Disney cruise is nothing short of magical.
