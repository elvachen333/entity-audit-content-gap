Now Is the Perfect Time to Sail

There’s a lot happening with Disney Cruise Line
 right now, which makes it a great time to start planning a cruise vacation! Bluey and Bingo now appear onboard the 
Disney Dream
 with interactive activities and dance parties. The experience will also be available on the 
Disney Wish
 beginning in June 2026.

The past few years have brought major updates and new destinations. In June 2024, Disney Cruise Line opened its second island destination, 
Lookout Cay at Lighthouse Point
. The Disney Dream also received several upgrades, including updates to Disney’s Oceaneer Club, a new funnel suite, a new concierge lounge and a refreshed spa.

Disney Cruise Line has also expanded its fleet. The 
Disney Treasure
, the second Wish Class ship, launched in December 2024 with adventure-inspired design, themed lounges, including the ghostly 
Haunted Mansion Parlor
 and a new Rotational Dining restaurant, Plaza de Coco.

In November 2025, the 
Disney Destiny
 debuted from Fort Lauderdale, sailing to the Bahamas and Western Caribbean with stops at Disney Castaway Cay and Disney Lookout Cay.

And just since March, the 
Disney Adventure
 began sailing from Singapore, with “Magic at Sea” cruises and brand-new themed areas inspired by Disney, Pixar and Marvel.
