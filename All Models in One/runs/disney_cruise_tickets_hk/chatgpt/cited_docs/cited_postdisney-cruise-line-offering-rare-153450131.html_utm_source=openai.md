## **Disney Cruise Line offering 50% off third and fourth guests deal**

Now through Oct. 12, Disney Cruise Line is offering a rare 50% off deal for select itineraries sailing between October 2025 and March 2026.

The cruise line is inviting vacationers to share the magic with family and friends by offering 50% off third and fourth passengers in a stateroom with the purchase of two full-fare guests in the same stateroom. For many families, that means kids can sail for half price.

Advertisement

Advertisement

The new offer is even valid for existing reservations that meet the eligibility criteria. If you’ve booked a Disney cruise on one of the following ships this fall or winter, call Disney Cruise Line to add the offer to your existing reservation.

**[Related: Disney Cruise Line passengers share urgent Lookout Cay warning](https://www.yahoo.com/lifestyle/articles/disney-cruise-line-passengers-share-155400902.html)**

This Disney Cruise Line discount offer is valid for select sailings on the Disney Magic, Disney Wonder, Disney Dream, Disney Fantasy, and Disney Wish between October 3, 2025 and March 30, 2026.

The offer is valid for stateroom categories 04A through 11C and cannot be combined with any other offers or discounts (including Disney Cruise Line’s guaranteed staterooms with restrictions offer). Concierge staterooms and suites are not eligible for the discount.

## **Other discounts available for Disney cruises in 2025**

If you’re looking to cruise before October or in a stateroom category that isn’t eligible for the 50% off discount, you may still be able to find a deal on a Disney cruise this year. Right now, Disney Cruise Line is also offering cruise fare discounts of up to 30% on certain stateroom categories on select itineraries in 2025.

Discounted Disney cruise fares of up to 30% are currently available for certain stateroom categories on select 2025 sailings from Port Canaveral and Fort Lauderdale in Florida; Southampton, England; San Juan, Puerto Rico; and Galveston, Texas.

Most of the current offers are for Verandah staterooms on itineraries sailing in August, September, and October, but some Very Merrytime holiday cruises are included, too. Discounts are even available on certain Halloween on the High Seas sailings.
