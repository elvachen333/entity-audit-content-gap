How the $250 Onboard Credit Works

What You Can Use It For

One $250 USD onboard credit will automatically appear on the stateroom account and can be used onboard toward shipboard services, merchandise, food and beverage, or port adventures.

Onboard Credit Restrictions

Nonrefundable and nontransferable

Cannot be redeemed for cash in whole or in part

Can only be used during the sailing booked with this offer

💡 Tip:

Use your onboard credit toward Port Adventures or specialty dining you were already planning to book—this is one of the easiest ways to turn this deal into real savings instead of impulse spending.

Can This Offer Be Combined With Other Discounts?

No. This offer cannot be combined with any other offers or discounts, including guaranteed staterooms with restrictions. Disney Vacation Points also cannot be used to book this offer.
