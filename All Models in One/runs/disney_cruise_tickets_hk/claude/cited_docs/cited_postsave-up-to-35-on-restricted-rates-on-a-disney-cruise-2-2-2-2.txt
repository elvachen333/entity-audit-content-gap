Important details on Inside Category with Restrictions (IGT), Oceanview Category with Restrictions (OGT), and Verandah Category with Restrictions (VGT)

Restricted rates apply to new bookings only. Existing reservations cannot be modified to a lower rate with a Restricted Fare.

Any payment paid on the reservation is non-refundable and non-transferable.

Full payment is required at the time of booking for all guests and is non-refundable and non-transferable. No courtesy holds.

Full legal names of all guests are required at time of booking as no name changes are allowed.

Sail date, cruise itinerary and stateroom category changes are not permitted after payment in full.

Rates and stateroom availability are very limited and is subject to change without notice.

Rate does not include gratuities or 
Port Adventures
.

A Restricted Rate guarantees you a stateroom within the selected stateroom type: Inside, Oceanview or Verandah. At their discretion, Disney Cruise Line will assign a stateroom.

Inside Stateroom with Restrictions (category IGT) – Guarantees an Inside Stateroom or better stateroom.

Oceanview Stateroom with Restrictions (category OGT) – Guarantees an Oceanview Stateroom or better stateroom.

Verandah Stateroom with Restrictions (category VGT) – Guarantees a Verandah Stateroom or better stateroom.

Category VGT (Verandah Guaranteed) staterooms may have a whitewall verandah and can accommodate a maximum of up to four guests depending on availability.

Rates are in U.S. dollars.

There is typically more availability in the Restricted Guarantee categories if there are only 2 or 3 people per stateroom.

There may not be more than one stateroom available for your party size even if you need two staterooms to accommodate all guests. Locations requests are not permissible.

The stateroom offer 
cannot
 be combined with any other discounted or promotional offer from Disney including Military rates, Florida Resident rates, etc
