Easyship

**Easyship** is a multi-carrier shipping software and logistics API platform that connects [eCommerce](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E-commerce "E-commerce") merchants, marketplace sellers and enterprise brands to hundreds of couriers and shipping services worldwide.[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-:1-1)[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-2)

The free shipping aggregator allows individuals and businesses to compare rates and find the cheapest way to ship a package with pre-negotiated volume discounts. Users can buy and print shipping labels, schedule carrier pickups, track shipments, calculate cross-border tax and duty fees, manage returns and add shipping insurance.

Easyship was launched in December 2014 in [Hong Kong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong "Hong Kong")[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-3)[\[4\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-4) before expanding to [Singapore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Singapore "Singapore") in November 2016.[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-:2-5)[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-6) It entered the North American market in the United States and Canada in July 2017. Today, Easyship operates internationally across the Americas, Europe and the Asia-Pacific region with offices in New York, London, Toronto, Bangalore, Taiwan, Melbourne and Hong Kong.

## Company history

Easyship was founded in 2014 by Tommaso Tamburnotti, Augustin Ceyrac, and Paul Lugagne Delpon to address the challenges of international shipping and simplify fulfillment for eCommerce merchants. It was launched at the [Hong Kong Science and Technology Park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hong_Kong_Science_and_Technology_Parks_Corporation "Hong Kong Science and Technology Parks Corporation") and expanded to Singapore in late 2016, before relocating its headquarters to the United States in mid-2017.[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-7)

In 2020, Easyship was accepted into the [Shopify](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shopify "Shopify") Plus Certified App Program (now known as the Shopify Certified Technology Partner Program), recognizing its reliability, scalability, and integration quality for high-volume merchants using Shopify's eCommerce platform. Easyship is also an [eBay](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/EBay "EBay") Shipping and Fulfillment Partner and in 2025 became an [Amazon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amazon_(company) "Amazon (company)") Preferred Multi-Channel Integrator, by integrating [Amazon Buy Shipping](https://sell.amazon.com/tools/buy-shipping) and Amazon Business order support for [Seller Fulfilled Prime (SFP)](https://sell.amazon.com/programs/seller-fulfilled-prime) and [Fulfilled By Merchant (FBM)](https://sell.amazon.com/programs/fulfilled-by-merchant) sellers in the US, UK and Europe.[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-8)

Easyship has built native shipping integrations with [Shopify](https://apps.shopify.com/easyship), WooCommerce, eBay, Amazon, Squarespace, BigCommerce, Magento, Adobe, WIX, Walmart, Shein, Temu, Etsy and TikTok Shop, to provide multi-channel shipping support for merchants.[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-9)

## eBay international shipping program

Easyship's [application programming interface](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/API "API") (API) has been used in several shipping and fulfillment services on eBay's global marketplace, including label generation, cross-border compliance and parcel tracking in the US. It has also supported domestic and international shipments in Canada and Australia since 2019. In April 2023, Easyship announced the expansion of its partnership with eBay to support [eBay International Shipping](https://www.ebay.com/help/selling/shipping-items/setting-shipping-options/ebay-international-shipping-program), a newly launched program that provides additional protections for eBay sellers. This allowed buyers to access more regional and express shipping solutions for international destinations through eBay's platform.[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-10)

## Shipping for crowdfunding campaigns

Easyship also supports shipping for crowdfunding platforms including [Kickstarter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kickstarter "Kickstarter")[\[11\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-11) and [Indiegogo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indiegogo "Indiegogo"),[\[12\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-12) supporting the delivery of campaign products to project backers all over the world.[\[13\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-13)[\[14\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Easyship#cite_note-14)
