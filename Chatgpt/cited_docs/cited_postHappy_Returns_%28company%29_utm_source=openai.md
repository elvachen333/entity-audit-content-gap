Happy Returns LLC

**Happy Returns LLC** is an American software and [reverse logistics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reverse_logistics "Reverse logistics") company that works with online merchants to handle product returns. Purchased items can be returned in person without boxes or labels at third-party locations known as "Return Bars" including The UPS Store, [Staples Inc.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Staples_Inc. "Staples Inc."), and Ulta Beauty stores,[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-vogue-1) with specific locations searchable on Happy Returns’ website. Consumers can receive their refunds without packaging items, printing and affixing labels, or waiting for mailed packages to reach their destination.[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-booming-2)

The company facilitates returns for many brands, including [Levi's](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Levi_Strauss_&_Co. "Levi Strauss & Co."), Revolve, [Everlane](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Everlane "Everlane"), [Lands' End](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lands'_End "Lands' End"), [Gymshark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gymshark "Gymshark").[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-apnews-3) [Amazon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amazon_(company) "Amazon (company)") later developed a similar program in conjunction with [Kohl's](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kohl's "Kohl's") stores to accommodate its shoppers.[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-booming-2)

Happy Returns aggregates and ships returns from its return locations to warehouse hubs, where returns are sorted, processed, and dispositioned, and then bulk shipped back to merchant warehouses.\[ _[citation needed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed "Wikipedia:Citation needed")_\] Waste is reduced because shoppers do not need to use a new box to ship out returns.[\[4\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-cnn-4)[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-theatlantic-5)

## History

David Sobie and Mark Geller co-founded Happy Returns in July 2015. The two met at NordstromRack.com/HauteLook, where they created and launched a similar program enabling shoppers to return online purchases to physical stores. Happy Returns opened its first Return Bar location in April 2016.[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-headache-6)

In June 2021, Happy Returns was acquired by [PayPal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PayPal "PayPal").[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-7)

In October 2023, it was announced Happy Returns had been acquired by [UPS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Parcel_Service "United Parcel Service") from PayPal[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-8) for $455 million.[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Happy_Returns_(company)#cite_note-9)
