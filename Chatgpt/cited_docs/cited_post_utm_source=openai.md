Shopper Experience

### The best returns experience for your shoppers

Give your shoppers a hassle-free returns experience by allowing shoppers to initiate returns on their own through a self-service returns portal located directly on your ecommerce store.

Returns Automation

### An automated returns process

Save your team from spending hundreds of hours manually processing your ecommerce returns and instead automate the process. Shoppers can initiate returns through your returns portal, which can be automatically approved or rejected based on your ecommerce store’s returns policy.

Returns Optimization

### Returns insights to improve your ecommerce store

ReturnLogic collects your returns data so you can see why shoppers are making returns in the first place. Leverage this data to make informed decisions about how to improve your product or post-purchase experience on your ecommerce store.

Returns Visibility

### The ability to accept & track all return types

Track the status and condition of all your returns, exchanges, and warranty claims in one place. This also includes items purchased from third-party sales channels like Amazon, eBay, etc.
