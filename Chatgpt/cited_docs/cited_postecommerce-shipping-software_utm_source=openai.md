### Integration with ecommerce platforms

A shipping software solution that offers real-time integration with your [ecommerce platforms](https://www.shopify.com/blog/best-ecommerce-platforms) can automatically import orders and update [stock levels](https://www.shopify.com/retail/stock-levels). This eliminates manual data entry and the potential for human error.

Look for features like automatic order retrieval from multiple sales channels, synchronization of stock numbers, and direct communication with your inventory management and [warehousing](https://www.shopify.com/blog/what-is-warehousing) systems to keep your ecommerce [business operations](https://www.shopify.com/blog/what-are-business-operations) cohesive and responsive to demand.

### Shipping volume and multi-carrier options

Whether your business sees seasonal spikes or is experiencing consistent growth, having software that scales with your shipping volume is essential. Seek out multi-carrier shipping software that facilitates rate comparisons. This can lead to optimal delivery times and cost savings, especially when the software includes features that automate the selection process based on pre-set preferences for price, speed, or [shipping carrier](https://www.shopify.com/blog/shipping-carrier) reliability.

### Cost management and discounted shipping rates

Efficient shipping cost management directly impacts your bottom line. Look for software for ecommerce that calculates shipping rates and provides access to discounted rates through pre-negotiated carrier agreements. Features that offer transparent rate comparisons and suggest less expensive alternatives without compromising delivery quality can significantly reduce shipping expenses.

### Support for international shipping and compliance

Global reach requires a shipping solution adept at handling international regulations and paperwork. The right software will automate customs forms and [duties](https://www.shopify.com/blog/duties-taxes) calculations, provide necessary tax documentation, and ensure adherence to international shipping laws. This kind of support minimizes the risk of customs [shipping delays](https://www.shopify.com/retail/shipping-delays) and can make the difference between a package arriving on time or getting stuck at the border.

### Streamlined shipping workflows and label printing

A shipping platform that simplifies workflows can dramatically speed up the packing and dispatching process. Advanced software should offer batch label printing, integration with printers and scales for accurate weight capture, and tracking directly in the dashboard. These features provide [faster fulfillment](https://www.shopify.com/blog/faster-fulfillment), save time, and reduce errors, allowing staff to focus on order accuracy and [customer service](https://www.shopify.com/blog/customer-service) rather than manual input tasks.

### Customer service and returns management

Effective management of customer service issues—including quick resolution of non-delivery reports (NDRs) and streamlined [returns management](https://www.shopify.com/blog/returns-management)—is essential for [customer retention](https://www.shopify.com/blog/customer-retention-strategies). Shipping software that includes an integrated returns portal, automated communications for status updates, and straightforward return label generation can improve the customer experience, even when issues arise.

## Ecommerce shipping software FAQ

### What is ecommerce shipping software?

Shipping software automates and manages the order fulfillment process for online businesses, including label printing, inventory tracking, and carrier integration.

### Which shipping company is best for ecommerce?

The best shipping companies for ecommerce depend on specific business needs, but major shipping carriers like UPS, FedEx, and USPS are commonly used for their reliability and range of shipping services.

### Does Shopify offer ecommerce shipping software?

Yes. Shopify offers integrated shipping software solutions within its platform, allowing businesses to manage orders, print shipping labels, reduce shipping costs, and track packages directly from their Shopify dashboard.
