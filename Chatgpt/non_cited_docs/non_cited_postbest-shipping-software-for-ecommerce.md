## **What’s the best shipping software for eCommerce in 2026?**

For 2026, top ecommerce shipping tools include Easyship, ShipStation, Shippo, Ordoro, and ShippingEasy.

Here are the 5 top ecommerce shipping software options for store owners:

### **1\. Easyship**

**Best for:** International shipping and cross-border logistics.

**Overview:**

Easyship is a global shipping platform that helps eCommerce sellers access over 250 international and local couriers. It’s particularly powerful for businesses looking to scale internationally without the complexity typically associated with cross-border commerce.

**Key Features:**

- **Smart shipping rates at checkout:** Shows dynamic, real-time shipping rates, taxes, and duties to buyers based on their location.
- **Massive courier network:** Connects sellers with top couriers like DHL, Aramex, SF Express, and regional services worldwide.
- **Tax and duty automation:** Automatically calculates duties and taxes during checkout, providing transparency for international buyers.
- **Customs paperwork generator:** Automatically fills out customs documentation, reducing compliance errors.
- **Warehousing integration:** Connects to global fulfillment centers for distributed inventory and faster international delivery.
- **Offers** [**free membership**](https://app.easyship.com/signup) that allows up to 50 shipments per month, with additional packages for a higher shipment volume.

**Ideal for:** Mid to large-sized businesses or any seller planning to go global. Especially valuable for Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Amazon sellers.

### **2\. ShipStation**

**Best for:** Multichannel sellers and high-volume order management.

**Overview:**

ShipStation is one of the most widely adopted shipping software tools in the industry. Known for its deep integrations and flexibility, it is a go-to solution for businesses managing multiple storefronts and sales channels.

**Key Features:**

- **Over 100 integrations:** Supports platforms like Amazon, eBay, Etsy, Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, Walmart, and more.
- **Robust automation rules:** Automatically assign shipping methods, print labels, or tag orders based on custom criteria.
- **Batch processing:** Print hundreds of labels at once, saving time for larger operations.
- **Branded tracking and emails:** Customize your post-purchase experience with branded notifications.
- **Inventory syncing:** Syncs order and inventory data across multiple platforms to prevent overselling.
- Includes free trial period and a starter membership priced at $9.99 for 50 shipments per month.

**Ideal for:** Merchants who sell across several platforms and marketplaces, especially those with in-house fulfillment teams needing efficiency and automation.

### **3\. Shippo**

**Best for:** Startups and small eCommerce brands seeking simplicity.

**Overview:**

Shippo is known for its user-friendly interface and cost-effective pricing model. It’s a great entry point for businesses that want to optimize shipping without a steep learning curve or complex setup.

**Key Features:**

- **Free plan available:** Includes basic features like multi-carrier support and discounted shipping labels.
- **40+ carrier integrations:** USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, and regional carriers.
- **Pre-filled customs forms:** Supports international shipping with automated document generation.
- **API for developers:** Useful for custom workflows or adding shipping functionality to proprietary eCommerce systems.
- **Branded tracking and analytics:** Offers a customer-friendly tracking page and built-in shipping performance analytics.
- Offers a free plan with limited features and a beginner plan priced at $17 per month for up to 200 labels monthly.

**Ideal for:** Solo entrepreneurs, small stores, or developers wanting to embed shipping into custom applications.

### **4\. Ordoro**

**Best for:** Businesses that want combined shipping, inventory, and supplier management in one tool.

**Overview:**

Ordoro is more than just a shipping platform—it's an operations hub for scaling brands. It’s ideal if you’re juggling inventory, purchase orders, shipping, and even dropshipping.

**Key Features:**

- **Inventory management:** Real-time stock level sync across sales channels.
- **Dropshipping support:** Assign SKUs to vendors and route orders automatically to suppliers.
- **Barcode scanning:** Streamlines warehouse picking and packing workflows.
- **Kitting and bundling:** Create product bundles and kits with automatic inventory deductions.
- **Shipping automation:** Rules for carrier selection, label generation, and custom workflows.
- Offers a free plan with limited features, monthly plan starts at $349 for inventory tracking.

**Ideal for:** Growing businesses needing more operational control across inventory and supplier relationships.

### **5\. ShippingEasy**

**Best for:** SMBs looking for shipping with built-in customer marketing tools.

**Overview:**

ShippingEasy combines eCommerce shipping with email marketing, creating a powerful post-purchase engagement platform. It's especially suited for businesses that want to build loyalty and drive repeat purchases.

**Key Features:**

- **Shipping discounts:** Excellent rates for USPS and UPS.
- **Built-in CRM:** Track customer purchase history and behavior.
- **Email marketing automation:** Create campaigns for abandoned cart recovery, product reviews, and upselling.
- **Customer segmentation:** Target segments based on purchase frequency, order size, and geography.
- **Returns portal:** Allows customers to initiate returns with branded pages.
- Offers free shipping of up to 25 shipments a month, with their monthly paid tier starting at $19.99 for up to 200 shipments.

**Ideal for:** Small to medium-sized online stores that want to manage shipping and retain customers through targeted email marketing.

## **Benefits of eCommerce Shipping Software**

Here’s how it helps your eCommerce business:

- **Saves time**: Automate repetitive tasks like creating labels, sending emails, and updating tracking.
- **Cuts shipping costs**: Use software for your eCommerce store with shipping discounts and bulk label printing.
- **Improves accuracy**: No more manual data entry. The software pulls data directly from your store.
- **Better customer satisfaction**: Branded tracking pages and seamless integration build trust and loyalty.

## **How to Choose the Right Shipping Software**

Choosing the right shipping platform depends on your goals. Here’s how to make a smart choice:

- **Know your shipping volume**: Smaller stores may prefer simpler tools like Shippo, while higher-volume sellers might benefit from more advanced multi carrier shipping solutions.
- **Look at integrations**: Make sure it connects with your eCommerce fulfillment tools and accounting software.
- **Compare pricing**: Free plans often include basic features. Paid plans give you more carrier discounts and advanced functions.
- **Check customer support**: Fast help is crucial when your shipping operations are delayed.

## **eCommerce Shipping for Small Businesses**

**Running a small business** comes with limited time and resources, making efficient shipping crucial to growth. The right shipping software can help you streamline fulfillment, save money, and deliver a professional experience, without needing a large team. If you're a small or solo business owner:

- **Start with free or low-cost shipping app plans**: Many tools have entry-level plans with great functionality.
- **Use automation early**: Even a few shipping rules can save hours of work.
- **Focus on the customer experience**: Automated tracking emails and reliable delivery create a seamless customer experience.
- **Use reports**: Track your eCommerce shipping process, carrier performance, and cost savings.

## **Shipping Software vs. Fulfillment Software**

These terms often get confused. Here’s the difference:

- **Shipping software** helps you print labels, compare carrier rates, and manage shipping expenses.
- **Fulfillment software** handles the full order fulfillment process: inventory management, picking, packing, and shipping.

If you ship products from home or a small warehouse, multi carrier shipping software is enough. If you store inventory across multiple locations, you might need both.

## **Best Practices to Optimize Shipping**

Follow these tips to improve your eCommerce shipping strategy:

- **Use right-size packaging**: This reduces shipping costs and avoids damage.
- **Provide multiple shipping options**: Some customers want speed; others want lower delivery costs.
- **Set automation rules**: Choose the cheapest label automatically based on weight and location.
- **Make returns easy**: Include a return label and clear instructions to streamline the eCommerce shipping solution.
