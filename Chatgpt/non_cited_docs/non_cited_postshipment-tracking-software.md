## The benefits of shipment tracking software

- **Transparency between merchants and customers:** Shipment tracking software updates customers on the status of their packages and also proactively sends notifications. Both merchants and customers can view the status, facilitating transparency between both parties.

- **Performance tracking for continuous improvement:** The software can track performance metrics such as accuracy of rates, on-time delivery, and performance of each carrier. Tracking these metrics helps users identify potential problem areas and make data-driven decisions to improve performance.

- **Batch processing streamlines shipping workflow:** Batch processing lets users combine similar shipments and process them at the same time. These batches can be created on the basis of parameters such as destination, delivery preference, and shipping date.

## Typical features of shipment tracking software

- **Delivery record:** Document the number of goods delivered and where, along with a time stamp verifying time of delivery.

- **Status tracking:** Monitor the routing information of the shipment in real time.

- **Alerts/notifications:** Receive and send update notifications automatically via email or SMS.

- **Website integration:** Integrate the software with third-party websites or applications including carrier sites and existing order management software.

## Considerations when purchasing shipment tracking software

- **Integrations with carriers from around the world:** Check whether a product offers integration with multiple shipping carriers. This will provide the flexibility to select the carrier that provides the most cost-effective shipping option for each order. Integration with local carriers also allows businesses to offer options such as same-day or next-day delivery to more customers.

- **Availability of mobile app:** Look for shipment tracking software that offers a mobile application. A mobile app can be used by delivery agents of freight companies to find the best route and obtain proof of delivery with a digital signature.

## Relevant shipment tracking software trends

- **Internet of things (IoT) empowers shipment tracking:** IoT lets businesses track the movement of a parcel from the distribution center to the customer’s address. IoT sensors can track the shipment's location via GPS to provide real-time updates.

- **Adoption of bots is rising to transform user experience:** Chatbots are transforming the shipping industry’s customer-facing operations when it comes to delivery scheduling, shipment tracking, and delivery-related inquiries. For example, [UPS offers chatbot assistance](https://www.ups.com/us/en/services/tracking/chatbot.page) for its My Choice clients that performs tasks such as tracking a shipment, changing an order, and requesting a delivery.

[Shipment tracking software](https://www.capterra.com/shipment-tracking-software/) is software designed for eCommerce and freight businesses to keep their customers informed of the status and whereabouts of their orders.

This type of software captures data generated by multiple carriers and automatically sends live updates to users and their customers. The software displays the origin and destination information, along with the delivery schedule, estimated arrival date, method of delivery, and tracking number. Customers are automatically updated if there is a change in delivery status.
