## The shipping software landscape: tools, trends, and what to look for

Shipping software has come a long way from basic label printing. Today, it’s the **backbone of e-commerce fulfillment**, connecting checkout, carriers, tracking, and returns into one smooth flow.

But many merchants still rely on disconnected tools or carrier portals. Shipping, returns, and support often live in separate systems, increasing manual work, delays, and customer frustration. As parcel volumes grow and expectations rise, this fragmentation becomes a blocker to scale.

That’s why modern shipping software is shifting toward [Connected Shipping](https://www.sendcloud.com/connected-shipping-guide/): a fully integrated experience that links every step of the journey — from order to doorstep to return. The right platform doesn’t just reduce effort, it gives you automation, real-time insights, and the kind of delivery experience that keeps customers coming back.

And with more platforms on the market than ever, it’s never been more important to compare based on what really matters: performance, automation, post-purchase experience, and fit for your business model.

## What to look for in your best shipping software

Not all shipping platforms are built the same. Some focus on label generation. Others handle tracking. A few try to cover it all, but with trade-offs in automation, analytics, or support.

To find the right fit, you need to dig into **how each tool handles the full shipping journey**: from the moment an order is placed to when it’s delivered (or returned).

Here’s what to compare:

### 🔗 Carrier coverage & flexibility

- How many carriers are supported — locally and internationally?
- Can you connect your own contracts, or only use pre-negotiated rates?
- Is there smart routing to choose the best carrier based on price, speed, or destination?

### 🔌 Integration with your stack

- Which shop systems, marketplaces, WMS/ERP tools are supported?
- How easy is setup — plug-in, API, or custom?
- Does it sync order data, update statuses, and push tracking automatically?
- How many shops or channels can you connect and are there limits in lower-tier plans?

### ⚙️ Automation & workflows

- Can you batch-print labels or set rules by country, weight, or SKU?
- Does it support pick-pack-ship flows, barcode scanning, or packing slips?
- Is the dashboard intuitive for warehouse or fulfillment teams?

### 📦 Post-purchase tracking & returns experience

- Is tracking real-time and branded with your logo, colors, and language? Can you send notifications via different channels?
- Can customers access a portal for self-service returns?
- Does the platform reduce “Where is my order?” tickets with proactive updates?

### 📊 Insights & performance reporting

- Does it show cost per shipment, carrier delays, or refund claims?
- Can you compare performance across time, carriers, or countries?
- Are dashboards actionable, exportable, and built for operators?

### 🌍 Localization & cross-border support

- Are customs forms, HS codes, and local rules handled automatically?
- Can you show delivery options in local language and currency?
- Is the tool adapted to regional carriers and delivery methods?

### 📈 Scalability & business fit

- Is it built for startups, SMBs, or enterprise, or flexible across sizes?
- Can it handle thousands of orders, multiple warehouses, and new markets?
- Will you outgrow the tool as your business evolves?

### 💸 Pricing & value

- Is there a free plan or flexible tiers?
- How does pricing scale with order volume, carriers, or features used?
- Are automation or branding features locked behind higher plans?

### 🧑‍💻 Support & onboarding

- What support is offered — live chat, help docs, account manager?
- How fast is setup, and is there onboarding help?
- Is the interface easy to use across teams?

### 🔒 Security, reliability & compliance

- Is the platform GDPR-compliant and built for data privacy?
- What’s the uptime track record — and how are outages handled?
- Are there protocols for backups, failover, and data recovery?

## FAQ: Shipping software comparison

### [Is there free shipping software for e-commerce?](https://www.sendcloud.com/shipping-software-comparison/\#)

Yes. Some tools like Sendcloud, Shipstation and Shippo offer free tiers. However, advanced features such as branded tracking, returns portals, or automation may be limited or only available on paid plans.

### [What is the best shipping software for small businesses?](https://www.sendcloud.com/shipping-software-comparison/\#)

If you’re a small business, look for shipping software that offers easy setup, automation, and flexible carrier options without high costs. Sendcloud, Shipstation, and Shippo all offer SMB-friendly plans — but only Sendcloud combines automation, tracking, and returns in a single platform for growing EU-based businesses.

### [What is the best shipping software for Shopify?](https://www.sendcloud.com/shipping-software-comparison/\#)

Shopify integrates with many shipping tools, including Sendcloud, Shipstation, and AfterShip. If you’re selling in Europe, Sendcloud stands out with full Shopify integration, EU carrier coverage, branded tracking, and multi-language returns — all optimized for Shopify workflows.

### [Which shipping software works best in Europe?](https://www.sendcloud.com/shipping-software-comparison/\#)

Sendcloud, MyParcel, and nShift are best suited for European merchants. Sendcloud supports 160+ EU carriers, full customs documentation, and multi-language tracking and returns, making it ideal for cross-border growth.

### [Can I use my own carrier contracts with shipping software?](https://www.sendcloud.com/shipping-software-comparison/\#)

Yes. Platforms like Sendcloud, Shippo, and nShift allow you to use your own carrier rates. Others may require you to use pre-negotiated rates or limit access to certain carriers depending on your plan.

### [Does shipping software support returns management?](https://www.sendcloud.com/shipping-software-comparison/\#)

Not all platforms offer built-in returns. Sendcloud and AfterShip include branded, self-service returns portals. Others like Shippo or MyParcel provide only basic return label generation without customer-facing features.

### [What’s the difference between Sendcloud and Shipstation?](https://www.sendcloud.com/shipping-software-comparison/\#)

Shipstation is strong for US-based SMBs needing label automation and marketplace integrations. Sendcloud offers deeper automation, EU carrier coverage, branded tracking and returns — making it better for scaling across Europe.
