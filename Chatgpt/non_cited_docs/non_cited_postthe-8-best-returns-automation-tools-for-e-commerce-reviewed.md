What To Consider When Buying Returns Automation Software For Your Online Store?

- **Return automation capabilities**: Automated returns management tools simplify initiation, tracking, and processing – and other automations, such as return rules

### Paid Returns

💡 As [48% of consumers are happy paying for returns and there’s a 32% rise in paid returns since last year](https://www.zigzag.global/paid-returns), we’ve started recommending retailers to offer paid returns on their stores.

Paid returns are when you ask consumers to pay a small fee to return their product – and we’ve seen the results.

Even though some retailers are hesitant to charge their customers for returns, [paid returns have gained traction and are now considered more acceptable than they were in the past](https://www.zigzag.global/blogs/why-are-retailers-charging-for-returns).

We’ve even seen customers react positively to paid returns, as it serves as a price tag to test out a product and return it if they do not like it.

Here are a few reasons why you should offer paid returns:

- Returned costs are covered so your company is not on the losing end.
- Consumers can’t exploit the free returns policy to engage in wardrobing.
- Customers are likely to make better buying decisions.
