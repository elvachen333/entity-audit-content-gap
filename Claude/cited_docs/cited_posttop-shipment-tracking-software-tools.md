## The importance of efficient shipment tracking in modern logistics

Uncertainty about carrier performance and [lack of visibility](https://www.sendcloud.com/last-mile-visibility-ecommerce/) into shipping disruptions can be major pain points. A reliable delivery tracking system is essential for proactive shipment management, minimizing disruptions, and ensuring a seamless flow of goods. For a great customer experience, visibility is crucial.

Did you know that [1 in 4 customers](https://www.sendcloud.com/optimize-order-tracking/?__hstc=134586094.4c63c73f157d612c2c74ae61010a1188.1717663507704.1718375686443.1718607629447.10&__hssc=134586094.2.1718607629447&__hsfp=2291465737) won’t reorder if tracking isn’t provided? [Prioritizing transparency and automated communication](https://www.sendcloud.com/parcel-tracking-solution/) can help retain customers and stay ahead of the curve.

## What are shipment tracking software tools?

Shipment tracking software tools are smart systems that allow businesses and customers to monitor parcels from dispatch to delivery. They provide real-time updates, predictive insights, and automated communication to smooth out delivery bumps.

In essence, these tools transform the question “Where’s my parcel?” into a clear, traceable journey. Whether sending a small package or managing a fleet, shipment tracking software ensures everything runs smoothly.

## FAQ

### [Who can benefit from shipment tracking software?](https://www.sendcloud.com/top-shipment-tracking-software-tools/\#)

Any business that ships goods can benefit from shipment tracking software. Whether you’re an emerging e-commerce start-up, a bustling retail giant, or a logistics titan, this software ensures transparency and reliability in your supply chain. Managing large volumes of shipments manually can be a nightmare.

Additionally, your customers will appreciate it too, as [90% of shoppers](https://www.raconteur.net/customer-experience/why-parcel-tracking-is-more-important-than-you-think) check their parcel status at least once. Providing transparent and accessible tracking information puts your customers at ease, helping them know when to expect their parcels and increasing the likelihood of successful first delivery attempts.

### [What factors are used to evaluate package tracking software?](https://www.sendcloud.com/top-shipment-tracking-software-tools/\#)

When evaluating shipment tracking software tools for 2024, consider these key factors:

- Accuracy of tracking information
- Frequency of delivery updates
- Availability of customer support
- Cost-effectiveness of the solution
- Ability to integrate with existing systems and processes
- Robustness of analytics and reporting features for actionable insights

### [How do you choose the best shipment tracking software for your business?](https://www.sendcloud.com/top-shipment-tracking-software-tools/\#)

To choose the best shipment tracking software, follow these three key steps:

- **Identify your primary pain points:** Note if your issues are due to lack of visibility into shipment statuses, customer dissatisfaction from delayed deliveries, or inefficiencies in handling shipping exceptions.
- **Match these needs with the features offered:** Consider your business needs and budget in relation to the software’s pricing. Don’t forget to evaluate the scalability of the solution.
- **Opt for a trial or demo:** Whenever possible, arrange for a demo to ensure the software meets your expectations and can seamlessly integrate with your operations.
