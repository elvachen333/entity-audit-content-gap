What pre-purchase solutions does Loop offer?

Loop offers tools that build confidence before someone buys. Delivery Promise shows accurate delivery estimates upfront. Checkout+ offers paid returns coverage at checkout. Order Editing lets shoppers update orders themselves, like address changes, cancellations, and add-ons, without contacting support.

What post-purchase solutions does Loop offer?

Loop supports the full post-purchase experience, from exchanges and returns to tracking, risk, and operations. Brands use Loop to retain more revenue through exchange-first flows, reduce support volume with proactive tracking, and automate decisions that protect margin.

What is Checkout+ from Loop?

Checkout+ lets shoppers add returns coverage at checkout. They pay a small fee, get a free return later, and your brand can offset return costs and protect margin.
