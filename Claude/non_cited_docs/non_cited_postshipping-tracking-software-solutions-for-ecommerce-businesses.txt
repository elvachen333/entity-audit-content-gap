Best Shipping Tracking Software Solutions for eCommerce Businesses in 2025

In the fast-paced world of eCommerce, customer satisfaction is key. Customers are most concerned about not knowing where their order is. From the moment they place an order until it arrives, they expect complete transparency. That’s why shipping tracking software has become essential for online businesses aiming for growth and success.

In the past, a simple "shipped” notification was sufficient. However, today’s customers demand live updates, accurate delivery estimates, and consistent communication. The right tracking software not only provides peace of mind but also reduces support calls, streamlines operations, and enhances brand reputation. With many options on the market, choosing the best can be overwhelming. This blog helps you to know the 
best shipping tracking software solutions for ecommerce businesses
 and why they are important in 2025!

Why Shipping Tracking Software is Essential in 2025?

Today's shoppers don't just want to place an order; they want real-time tracking, updates via SMS or email, and an estimated delivery date. Companies that don't offer these features may lose customers to competitors who do.

Providing live order status for customers.

Reducing “Where is my order?” (WISMO) customer service calls.

Offering branded tracking pages for better marketing opportunities.

Improving customer trust and retention.

Integrating with multiple couriers worldwide.

Best 8 Shipping Tracking Software Solutions for eCommerce Businesses

Here are the best 8 shipping tracking software solutions for ecommerce businesses.

1. AfterShip

AfterShip is one of the best shipping tracking software solutions. It offers a wide range of features, including branded tracking pages, email and SMS notifications, and detailed reports. With over 900 carriers worldwide, it is extremely flexible. Businesses can also customize the tracking page to suit their brand, giving customers a professional and seamless experience.

2. ShipStation

ShipStation is another 
top shipping tracking software solutions
 for ecommerce businesses that simplifies order fulfillment, automates shipping tasks, and integrates with multiple carriers. It offers real-time tracking, branded notifications, discounted shipping rates, and seamless integration with platforms like Shopify, Amazon, and WooCommerce.

3. ParcelPanel

ParcelPanel is designed to make tracking pages attractive and user-friendly. It also sends automated tracking updates. It works well with platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce and allows businesses to customize their tracking system. Its main strength lies in providing a visually appealing, branded experience that enhances customer engagement.

4. Malomo

Malomo concentrates on the post-purchase journey by utilizing tracking as a marketing opportunity. It not only displays tracking updates but also enables businesses to add marketing messages, product recommendations, and customer surveys to the tracking page. This way, it transforms a straightforward tracking page into a customer engagement tool.

5. Route

Route is a renowned 
shipping tracking software solution for 2025
, helping both customers and sellers by offering protection for lost, stolen, or damaged parcels. It features a special "resolve" function that simplifies the handling of these issues. One of its unique features is “visual tracking,” which allows you to see your package's journey more engagingly and interestingly. This not only provides reassurance but also makes tracking parcels a more enjoyable experience.

6. EasyPost

Easy Post is an ideal choice for businesses seeking full control and customization of their shipping and tracking system. It provides a flexible API that developers can integrate directly with their existing systems. Although it may require some technical expertise to set up, it’s perfect for companies shipping large volumes or with specific shipping requirements. It’s especially useful for developers seeking powerful and scalable tracking software.

7. Shippo

Shippo is a full-service 
shipping tracking software solution for ecommerce businesses
 that simplifies handling shipments for businesses. It partners with multiple shipping carriers and offers competitive rates. With Shippo, you can manage all your shipments from a single dashboard, making the process straightforward and organized. Its ease of use and affordability make it a great choice for businesses of all sizes.

8. SendCloud

SendCloud is a popular shipping tracking software solution for 2025  in Europe, helping businesses streamline shipping and tracking. It connects with over 100 carriers and provides automatic tracking updates, branded tracking pages, and customer service tools. Since it supports international shipping, it’s well-suited for businesses with global customers.

Final Words

In today’s competitive online market, delivering a great experience after purchase is crucial. Using the best shipping tracking software helps you stay on top of things, communicate effectively, and maintain a strong brand image. It transforms standard deliveries into opportunities to impress customers and build loyalty.
