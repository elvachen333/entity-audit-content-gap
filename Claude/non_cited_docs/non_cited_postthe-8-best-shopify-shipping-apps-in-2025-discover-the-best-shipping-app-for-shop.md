# The 8 Best Shopify Shipping Apps in 2025: Discover the Best Shipping App for Shopify

Rebekah

\|

March 30, 2025

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/61b51cc08207921f523c5704/67ea0edbde2a97896fc5bffc_box1.png)

Shipping: it's one of the most important things you’ll need to manage as an ecommerce business owner. In fact, around [50% of customers](https://www.reutersevents.com/supplychain/ecommerceretail/competitive-pressure-amazon-brings-average-online-purchase-delivery-time-down-2-3) in the US say they won’t even bother purchasing from a company again if they fail to keep their shipping promises. Unfortunately, shipping challenges can be complex – that’s where the best Shopify shipping apps come in.

These digital tools are designed to help organizations streamline their fulfillment processes. They can help you find the right logistics partner, based on your budget or your customer’s location, track orders from point A to B, and so much more.

Although Shopify does have it’s own shipping solutions (and a great fulfillment network), adding an app into the mix can really take your fulfillment strategy to the next level.

So, which apps are worth using in 2025? Here are our top picks.

## The Benefits of the Best Shopify Shipping Apps

Before we explore some of the best Shopify shipping apps, you might be wondering why it’s worth using one. After all, the more apps you add to your Shopify store, the more bloat you’ll deal with (not to mention extra costs). But the best shipping app for Shopify is a valuable tool.

The right app gives you access to a host of benefits, such as:

- **Streamlined order processing:** With a shipping app, you can automate tasks like order imports, label generation, and carrier selection, slashing manual errors and speeding up fulfillment. ​Some tools even offer inventory management capabilities.
- **Access to multiple carriers:** Shipping apps allow you to compare rates from USPS, FedEx, UPS, DHL, and more, reducing logistics costs. Most tools will even give you insights into real-time shipping rates, which you can share with shoppers.
- **Customizable shipping rules:** Tailor shipping options based on product weight, location, or order value, so you can address the preferences of different customers. Some solutions also support dedicated rules for international or global shipping.
- **Boosted branding:** Many shipping apps allow business owners to customize labels, packing slips, and tracking emails to maintain a consistent brand image throughout the shipping journey. ​Plus, they make it easy to keep customers up-to-date on orders with tracking tools.
- **Improved customer satisfaction:** With shipping apps, you can deliver accurate estimates, reliable tracking, and flexible options, to customers, boosting your chances of increased customer loyalty, and higher sales.

## The Top Shopify Shipping Apps for 2025

Now, let’s dive into some of the best Shopify shipping apps available to merchants in 2025. There are literally hundreds of options to choose from. We’ve based this list on a careful consideration of the overall features an app offers, as well as its reputation among Shopify companies.

1. Shippo

![A person smiling with her hands on her faceAI-generated content may be incorrect.](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/61b51cc08207921f523c5704/67ea0eeecf83c62c568affe9_AD_4nXeFaHuzHRgujQ3r6tJ4uNWZPl0LKhx9YZL5Nko0BOLRm6Y3l-RRdkW3admJChUJXzyFMeRvRi3YiCJoETM1RJ5tGm8yFZsLZyedE0zpd5_6zgp6ukv7KM9oivCYsdfZkryng2dihJBqAmwqvIV8rQ.png)

Easily one of the most popular shipping apps available to Shopify merchants, [Shippo](https://www.1800d2c.com/tool/shippo) makes order fulfillment quick and simple. It seamlessly integrates with your Shopify checkout, granting instant access to discounted rates from leading carriers like FedEx, USPS, and DHL.

There’s even a free plan for beginners, which makes Shippo a great tool for smaller companies with a limited budget for add-ons and applications. Top features include:

- **Customizable Branding:** Create personalized packing slips and tracking pages that reflect your brand's identity.​
- **Automated Tracking:** Keep customers informed with real-time tracking updates, enhancing transparency and trust.​
- **Shipping Automation:** Set up rules to automate tasks like label creation and order importing, reducing manual intervention.​

**The Pros and Cons**

Shippo is affordable, user-friendly, and it offers access to shipping solutions from dozens of carriers worldwide. However, it’s worth noting that the free plan does limit access to advanced features, like the ability to connect your own carrier accounts.

## Choosing the Best Shopify Shipping Apps

Choosing the best Shopify shipping apps can be difficult – particularly when there are so many different options available. If you’re struggling to make the right choice, here are a few things we recommend looking for in the best apps:

- **Multi-Carrier Support**: An effective shipping app should integrate with multiple carriers, such as USPS, FedEx, UPS, and DHL. This flexibility should help you choose most cost-effective and reliable shipping options for each order you process.
- **Real-Time Rate Shopping**: Providing customers with accurate shipping costs at checkout is vital. Apps that offer real-time rate comparisons enable you to display the best available shipping options to every customer, reducing cart abandonment.
- **Automation:** Automation minimizes manual errors and saves time. Look for apps that allow you to set custom shipping rules based on criteria like weight, location, order value, and shipping method. The less time you spend managing shipping, the more you can focus on growth.
- **Shipping Analytics**: Access to detailed shipping analytics enables you to monitor performance, identify cost-saving opportunities, and optimize your shipping strategy. Insights into shipping costs, delivery times, and carrier performance are really helpful for merchants.
- **Scalability:** As your business grows, your shipping app should accommodate increased order volumes and expanded shipping needs. Ensure the app can scale with your business without struggling with lag or accuracy issues.
