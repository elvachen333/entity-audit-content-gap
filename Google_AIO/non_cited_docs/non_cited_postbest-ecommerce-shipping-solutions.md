One of the most crucial aspects of managing an online company is shipping. Customers who are dissatisfied with their [shipping](https://flowium.com/blog/ecommerce-shipping/) experience could write unfavorable reviews that harm your company’s reputation.

Although you can distribute your products to your clients using the [shipping](https://flowium.com/blog/ecommerce-shipping/ "shipping") system in your CMS, shipping software is usually preferable. This is because all ecommerce shipping software was developed to save you time, money, and headaches by assisting you in resolving all shipping-related problems.

In this thorough guide, we’ll explore the **best shipping solutions for ecommerce** in 2026 to help you select the best option for your online store.

## Best Shipping Software for Ecommerce

Here, you can find a holistic list of the **best shipping platforms for ecommerce** which is based on Flowium’s own expertise, since some of them are [our partners](https://flowium.com/partners/), as well as client reviews.

### [AfterShip](https://www.aftership.com/)

![Aftership logo](https://flowium.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/aftership.png)

AfterShip positions itself as the “#1 Post-Purchase Customer Experience Platform,” offering a full suite of tools designed to enhance the customer journey from shipment tracking to returns. This is an automated shipment tracking system that allows ecommerce companies to monitor their shipments all in one location. UPS, USPS, DHL, FedEx, Blue Dart, China Post, Aramex, Australia Post, TNT, and more are among the 1100+ carriers that brands can track through this **top ecommerce shipping platform**. Brands can get tracking information on a single, user-friendly dashboard and automatically import orders to AfterShip. Thanks to this solution, they can use real-time tracking information to address delivery delays as soon as possible, naturally elevating customer experience. The platform also allows planning and sending proactive delivery [notifications via SMS](https://flowium.com/lp/sms-service/) and email.

#### Features

The platform’s key features include:

- Customizable branded tracking pages
- Returns and warranty automation
- Shipping protection
- AI-powered delivery date estimates
- AI-powered email parser
- Shipping reviews
- Product recommendations

AfterShip excels in managing the post-purchase experience. It connects with 1,184 carriers globally and offers strong APIs and webhooks for smooth data flow and automation.

#### Integration and Compatibility

In addition to offering smooth interaction with ecommerce marketing platforms like Klaviyo, Yotpo, Gorgias, and Salesforce Commerce Cloud, AfterShip also interfaces with Shopify, Shopify Plus, NetSuite, Magento, and BigCommerce. There is no need for [third-party email services](https://flowium.com/blog/best-email-marketing-services/) because its products are developed internally to provide a consistent customer experience, such as integrated delivery alerts.

#### Target Market and Pricing

AfterShip serves a wide spectrum of ecommerce companies, from small start-ups to major corporations. The platform offers both public and enterprise plans, and seven-day free trials are offered. Premium subscriptions include advanced features like AI-powered EDD, unique connectors, and enterprise-level support, making them appropriate for a range of corporate requirements.

Overall, when billed monthly, the pricing range looks the following way:

- Essentials – $11/month (100 shipments);
- Pro – $119/month (2,000 shipments);
- Premium – $239/month (2,000 shipments);
- Enterprise – custom price.

For more information, see our comprehensive [AfterShip pricing overview](https://flowium.com/blog/aftership-pricing-overview/).

### [Malomo](https://gomalomo.com/)

![Malomo logo](https://flowium.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/malomo.png)

Malomo is a cloud-based shipment tracking technology that helps small to midsize ecommerce companies increase the value of their brands by giving customers delivery updates through marketing campaigns and white-label package tracking pages. Using buyer purchase information and carrier data, users can generate customized shipping emails and use behavior trends to guide their choices. This **top-notch shipping software for ecommerce** focuses on branded order tracking and analytics, turning the tracking process into a marketing opportunity.

Malomo’s unique selling proposition is its ability to turn order tracking into a branded experience that increases client loyalty and promotes repeat business. They assert that the order tracking page receives up to 20% of all website visitors, demonstrating the importance of this touchpoint.

#### Features

Malomo enables companies to send buyers scheduled shipping updates, monitor and manage delivery information across purchases, and create and evaluate post-purchase experiences with bespoke pages. [Ecommerce marketers](https://flowium.com/lp/our-services/) can use the platform to expedite customer engagement procedures for support requests, touchpoint engagements, repeat business, and other areas. Companies can also alert customers to possible problems that might arise during delivery.

Its key **_features_** include:

- Customizable tracking page creator
- Delivery issue monitoring
- Transactional notification control
- Shipping analytics
- Problem order detection

#### Integration and Compatibility

Malomo is particularly well-suited for Shopify stores, offering easy integration with various Shopify apps and marketing tools. However, it relies on third-party solutions, such as Loop Returns for returns management, and lacks an in-built shipping notification function. Numerous third-party platforms, including Shopify, Nosto, Zendesk, Justuno, Olark, Yotpo, Pixlee, and others, are integrated with Malomo. By sending specially designed emails, businesses may leverage Klaviyo integration to improve the shipping experience for consumers, boosting click-through rates and revenue across all processes.

#### Target Market and Pricing

Malomo targets growing brands. It offers both public and custom pricing plans and provides a free trial – 30 days.

![Target Market and Pricing of Malomo offering both public and custom pricing plans and provides a free trial - 30 days.](https://flowium.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/target-market-and-pricing.png)

### [Loop Returns](https://www.loopreturns.com/)

![Wonderment logo](https://flowium.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Loop-logo.jpg)

[Loop Returns](https://flowium.com/partners/wonderment/) (Wonderment) is a customer experience platform specifically designed for Shopify and Shopify Plus merchants. It focuses on enhancing the post-purchase experience, particularly in the areas of order tracking, shipping updates, and customer communication.

This is the **best shipping software for Shopify** merchants that helps [direct-to-consumer (DTC) firms](https://flowium.com/blog/dtc-marketing/) increase revenue, improve customer retention, and expedite shipment tracking. Simply put, Loop Returns provides upsell and cross-sell chances on tracking pages and helps you decrease confused support calls from customers who can’t find their orders. Loop Returns offers brands a reasonable cost of entry while maximizing their post-purchase potential.

![YouTube video](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/I_x5Ie4UcT0/hqdefault.jpg)

#### Features

To provide a seamless client experience, the platform provides branded order tracking pages, proactive shipping alerts, and customized shipment tracking email and [SMS campaigns](https://flowium.com/lp/sms-service/). Its key features include:

- Branded tracking page design
- Shipment notifications
- AI-powered delivery estimates
- Reporting & Analytics
- Email & SMS notifications
- One Click integrations

#### Integration and Compatibility

Loop Returns is primarily designed for Shopify and Shopify Plus platforms. Loop’s features require third-party integrations.

#### Target Market and Pricing

Loop Returns is designed for small to medium-sized businesses with a focus on growing Shopify brands.

Loop Returns offers three public plans.

The total number of shipments you make each month determines all prices. Three price categories with various features are available. The Starter, Premium, and Plus plans start at $99, $189, and $449 per month, respectively, and cover up to 2,000 shipments every month. They also give you a 10% discount if you use them for a full year. AI delivery estimates feature is not included in any plan.

![Shippo logo](https://flowium.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/shippo-1.png)

Finding the cheapest carriers for its clients is the main goal of the **top ecommerce shipping software** Shippo. It makes it simple for retailers to offer their goods for shipment anywhere in the world and provides them with access to low rates from major carriers. Shippo stands out in the market thanks to this model.

#### Features

Businesses can manage all of their shipping from a single location with Shippo’s user-friendly platform, which easily adjusts to your needs as your company expands. The robust API gives developers complete control and allows for specific integrations that suit certain workflows, making it ideal for marketplaces, ecommerce platforms, and larger businesses. Integrated features include consumer notifications, effective return handling, and real-time shipping tracking.

**_Features_**:

- Order management & tracking
- Shipment tracking
- Automated customer notifications
- Shipping label creation with the lowest possible rates from top global carriers
- Return labels generator
- Batch and automated workflows

#### Integration and Compatibility

You can compare prices and automate label printing, tracking, and returns using Shippo’s seamless integration with leading carriers including USPS, UPS, and FedEx. Shippo saves you time and money, regardless of how many parcels you send each month – 100 or 10,000.

#### Target Market and Pricing

The pricing structure offered by Shippo is tiered. Up to 30 labels can be used each month with its free Starter plan, while up to 10,000 labels can be used with its $19/month Professional plan. Larger companies can choose Premier options with limitless labels for a bespoke fee. An API with comparable tiers is also offered by Shippo. Small to medium-sized ecommerce companies are its target market, particularly those looking for scalable, reasonably priced shipping options.

![Easyship logo](https://flowium.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/easyship-1.png)

With the help of Easyship, a cloud-based technology, ecommerce companies can expedite the entire shipping process, from obtaining quotations to completing orders with a variety of carriers like FedEx, Singapore Post, DHL, and others. As the **best shipping service for ecommerce**, it can simplify your worldwide demands into a single solution and help you save up to 91% on shipping costs if your company operates internationally. Ecommerce companies adore Easyship’s user-friendly interface, but they also adore its exclusive shipping offers.

#### Features

Easyship helps companies print pre-filled address slips, make shipping/packaging labels, and automate or personalize shipping preferences depending on factors like price, size, courier, and more. To cut down on extra expenses, professionals can use the platform to check and compare quotations or prices in real time. It also enables users to manage product catalogs, schedule pickups, provide customers with shipment tracking information, and keep an eye on activities.

**_Features_**:

- Streamlined cloud-based shipping dashboard
- Discounted shipping rates, up to 91% off
- Duty and tax charges management for international orders
- Global [order fulfillment](https://flowium.com/blog/ecommerce-order-fulfillment/ "order fulfillment") & batch shipping
- Optimized store-checkout
- Shipping labels, packing slips, and customs declarations generator

#### Integration and Compatibility

Easyship makes it easier to integrate with a number of third-party shipping companies and ecommerce platforms, like FedEx, Aramex, Shopify, Magento, Amazon, and more.

#### Target Market and Pricing

Depending on the total number of shipments or users, the solution is offered for free and through monthly or yearly subscriptions. Easyship has multiple pricing options, including paid plans like Plus and Premier which give additional features and greater shipment allowances, as well as a free plan for small businesses that covers up to 50 shipments per month. Users simply pay for the delivery expenses and the plan they have selected, with discounts of up to 70% offered. Ecommerce businesses of all sizes looking for international shipping solutions are the main aim of Easyship.

## How to Choose the Best Shipping Solution for Ecommerce

For companies looking to increase productivity and customer satisfaction, **choosing ecommerce shipping platforms** for their requirements is an essential first step. You should look for the following features in a program:

- _Connectivity to Ecommerce Platforms_

Instantaneous integration for stock updates and order imports. Synchronization across various inventory, warehousing, and sales channels.

- _Scalability and Options for Multiple Carriers_

Multi-carrier capabilities to accommodate varying shipping volumes. Automated pricing comparisons to maximize carrier dependability, cost, and delivery time.

- _Cost Management_

Obtaining pre-arranged, reduced delivery prices. Clear rate comparisons and recommendations for more affordable options.

- _Compliance and International Shipping_

The automation of tax documentation, duties computations, and customs forms. Adherence to global regulations to avoid delays.

- _Simplified Procedures_

Features such as in-dashboard tracking, scale integration, and batch label printing. Quicker fulfillment procedures with fewer human mistakes.

- _Customer Service and Returns Management_

Tools for creating return labels and effectively addressing delivery concerns; an integrated returns portal and automated status updates.

## FAQ

**What is an ecommerce shipping solution?**

Ecommerce companies can automate shipping and tracking procedures and manage them with the use of shipping tracking solutions. It saves business owners time by doing away with the need to manually enter data across many platforms. It also makes things like figuring out shipping costs, making sure customs laws are followed, and printing labels easier.

**What are the benefits of using ecommerce shipping solutions?**

Ecommerce shipment tracking systems decrease support questions, increase customer happiness, and provide real-time order tracking. They reduce errors, improve operational efficiency, and streamline logistics. Additionally, they provide insights into delivery performance and assist create trust with clients.
