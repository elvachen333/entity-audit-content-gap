Why do you need a returns management software?

Without an efficient returns management process, customer returns can quickly become a nightmare for your e-commerce business. Did you know that, on average, returns can cost retailers 
27% of the original order value
? Plus, only 
30% of returned items
 ever get resold.

Curious about the latest trends in e-commerce returns?

One of the biggest issues is that many customers shop with the intention of returning some or all of their items. This forces businesses to expand warehouse space and hire more staff, leading to increased costs and lower profits.

As an enterprise retailer, you may have a larger budget for your returns process but you face an equally higher return rate. While a relaxed return policy can make or break sales in today's competitive market, it puts pressure on the operational efficiency of returns management, which eventually grows unsustainable in terms of reverse logistics, finances, and environmental responsibility.

Here's the good news though. Investing in the right returns management system can turn this challenge into an opportunity. The right tool can make your e-commerce business more profitable and set your brand apart in a crowded market without sacrificing resources and profitability.

The right returns management software can help your business:

Increase operational efficiency.
 Returns process automation reduces mistakes, optimizes resource allocation, and 
lowers costs
.

Drive revenue growth.
 Features like exchanges and gift cards are great for retaining sales, promoting upsell and cross-sell, and ensuring repeat purchases from customers who would instead be lost.

Optimize associated costs.
 Smartsupply chain solutions for returns inventory management and reverse logistics, such as circular returns and re-commerce,make operations much cheaper and more environmentally sustainable.

Increase
 
customer satisfaction

 With a seamless, one-click returns and exchange experience, retailers boost customer loyalty without sacrificing profit.

Generate valuable insights.
 Understanding the what, who, and why of returns and exchanges helps address common issues at the design, production, and marketing stages to prevent future post-purchase problems.

An efficient returns process is key to your online store’s success. It keeps operations smooth, maximizes the value of returned products, and helps you stay ahead in the competitive world of e-commerce.

How to choose the right returns management platform

Choosing the right e-commerce returns management solution can be overwhelming. To make the process easier for you, we’ve put together a checklist that highlights the key aspects to consider when choosing a returns software.

Integrations

Can the returns management solution integrate with your existing setup? Can it be integrated with your e-commerce platform and inventory system?

Analytics and reporting

How solid are the reporting features? They should make it easy for you to spot trends, improve your products, and make smart decisions about your returns strategy.

Customization

Is the software tailored to your specific needs? How smooth will the 
post-purchase experience
 be?

Scalability

Can the software grow with your business? Can it handle increased return volumes?

Pricing

What's the value for money? Are there different pricing options available? Have you checked both upfront costs and ongoing fees?

Support

Can you count on support during implementation and onboarding? Is there a dedicated customer success manager to speak to in case of any questions?

User-friendly interface

Is the interface intuitive and straightforward? Would the onboarding process require a lot of time and effort?

Automation capabilities

Can the software automate repetitive tasks? How much time can it save you?
